Who We Are

A high quality, custom-made vinyl pressing service that caters to any music lover. Looking to get your special someone the ultimate mixtape, a limited pressing of your band's first breakout album, or a compilation of your favorite Brazilian psychedelic rock? Well that’s why we’re here.

Who We Are

Jack Davis began listening to vinyl with his father from a young age. It became an all consuming passion in college. Soon he was at every swap meet and garage sale. Stacks of records piled on
his floor. A fixture in the local grunge rock scene in Allston, MA, his band realized they wanted to capture their sound uncompressed.

That’s when “Tailored Groove” was born. The late nights spent sipping PBR and listening to rare bootleg pressings of James Brown and the Famous Flames might actually have been worth something. No longer does Jack spend his nights in dimly lit basements. Although, still looking
like a struggling musician, he’s now much more. Starting with an Indiegogo campaign, his
company “Tailored Groove” raised $10,000 to buy the speciality equipment required for pressing their own records.

Our Process

We manufacture all of our vinyl records in the Unites States using vintage Hamilton record presses - quality machines that produce the finest sounding product. Our standard records weigh in at a generous 150 grams. Our album jackets are crafted to perfection from the best materials, such as full color printing using only non-toxic, vegetable-based, biodegradable inks for a rich, vibrant look.

Tailored Groove’s dedicated production staff performs a multitude of quality checks during the production process to ensure your tunes sound optimal when mastered and pressed into your custom-made vinyl record. From the moment you order the record, it takes about 16 weeks to deliver the product to your door.
