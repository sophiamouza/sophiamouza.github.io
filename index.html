Feel Your Favorite Tunes

Create your ideal playlist and our team will press it on a 12-inch vinyl record. Tailored Groove allows you to enjoy a physical copy and exceptional sound of your favorite music.

Welcome

Whether you are someone who wants to put together your most beloved 60s psychedelic rock tracks or get your band’s new album on a record, Tailored Groove is the place to do it. We offer limited record pressing services, print high-quality jackets featuring your artwork and provide sleeves to ensure your record is kept pristine.

How it Works

Come up with a list of songs and upload
your music files.
Create an album cover using your artwork
or our “Tailored Groove” template. Give the
record a final touch by writing text for an
album label.
Press “Checkout Now” and count down
the days till you can lower the needle on
your very own custom record.
