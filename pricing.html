Pricing

“Tailored Groove”
Default Jacket

“Tailored Groove” default jacket allows you to create a custom-made record quickly without having to spend time designing the cover. Just let us know your album’s name and we will get it pressed in no time.

Complete package includes one black 12-inch vinyl record, a plain white sleeve and a 12-inch default “Tailored Groove” jacket printed on 15pt whiteboards.

$30

Default “Tailored Groove” template that features your record title on the front cover and an automated track list on the back.

Single Jacket

If you have a photo or artwork that you would like to see on your album cover, the single jacket option is the choice for you. Upload the image to the website and get ready to feast your eyes on your very own custom record.

Complete package includes one black 12-inch vinyl record, a plain white sleeve and a 12-inch full color single jacket with your artwork printed on 15pt whiteboards.

$45

Single jacket that features your artwork on both, front and back covers. The track list is not automated and needs to be included in your work.

Double Jacket

Double jacket option is for some who cares about the image as much as they care about the music. They value the tangible. The physical. A memory they can hold onto for decades. Go ahead and upload your artwork, we can’t wait to see it!

Complete package includes one black 12-inch vinyl record, a plain white sleeve and a 12-inch full color double gatefold jacket with your artwork printed on 15pt whiteboards.

$60

Double jacket that features your artwork on front and back covers, as well as on both sides inside the gatefold. The track list is not automated and needs to be included in your work.
